# 01 — User Profiles

## Primary User — The Politically Motivated Taxpayer

A working adult between 35–65 with at least one IRA account.

- Politically engaged and motivated to take concrete financial action — not just symbolic action
- Not a financial advisor, but capable of looking up their taxable income
- Comfortable with a basic web form
- Wants to understand the real cost of their protest before committing
- Not looking for a full retirement plan — just one clear answer: "If I do this, here's what it will cost me"
- Afraid of making a decision that sounds noble but turns out to be financially irrational
- Wants to feel informed, not impulsive

> "I want to do something real. I just want to know what it's actually going to cost me."

---

## Secondary User — The Financially Curious Skeptic

Similar demographic but motivated more by curiosity than activism.

- May have a spouse pushing for action, or just want to understand the mechanics
- More analytical; will scrutinize the numbers and run multiple scenarios

> "Show me the math. I'll decide what it means."

---

## Out-of-Scope Users

- People who haven't made IRA contributions yet
- Users seeking comprehensive retirement planning
- Financial professionals seeking CPA-grade accuracy
- Non-US users
