# 04 — Project Statement

## What This Is

The **IRA Recharacterization Protest Calculator** is a free, open-source, client-side web application that helps US taxpayers understand the true financial cost of recharacterizing a Roth IRA contribution to a traditional IRA as an act of political protest.

It was built in response to federal policies under the Trump administration that many Americans find unconscionable — including aggressive ICE enforcement, attacks on democratic institutions, and the erosion of civil rights.

For taxpayers who want to take meaningful financial action but lack legal means to withhold taxes outright, IRA recharacterization is one of the few tools available **within the law**.

> This calculator does not tell you what to do. It tells you what it costs.

Every meaningful protest imposes a cost on the person making it. This tool makes that cost legible.

---

## What This Is Not

- This is **not** tax advice
- This is **not** financial planning software
- This is **not** affiliated with any political organization, party, or campaign

It is a transparent, single-purpose educational tool built by an independent developer.

---

## Principles

- No data is collected, stored, or transmitted. Ever.
- The code is open source and auditable.
- The math is documented and transparent.
- The tool has a point of view, but the numbers are honest.
